Formulas and Equations
======================

The formal expression of the formulas and equations used in the library are detailed here.

Economic Order Quantity (eoq)
-----------------------------

.. math::

    eoq_{0} = \sqrt \frac{2 \times R \times D}{HC}

where:
    R = Reorder Cost
    D = Demand
    HC = Holding Cost
